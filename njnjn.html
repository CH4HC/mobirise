Introduction
项目介绍

1

在城市小型住宅中,空间的规划尤为重要。在特定的空间内,如何保持功能的完整性,同时又要使空间最大化的利用,这一问题是很难把握的。本次报告以卫生间的整合利用为例，通过对空间的组合和分析，储藏空间的运用以及对住宅内部环境和自然环境的利用来尽可能的解决这个矛盾，提出一些概念上的方案与解析。

2

Space planning is particularly important in small-scale urban housing. In a specific space, it is difficult to grasp how to maintain the integrity of functions and maximize the use of space. This report takes the integrated utilization of toilet as an example, through the combination and analysis of space, the use of storage space and the use of the internal environment and natural environment of the residence to solve this contradiction as far as possible, and puts forward some conceptual solutions and analysis.

Illusion of choice

选择的幻觉

——

现实中，许多城市底层家庭的生活空间以及卫生条件十分简陋，狭小的空间显得非常拥挤。在我们平时的生活中，有许许多多家庭的生活还仅仅是处在温饱线上，但是，人生而平等，每一个人都应该有选择的权力，或是说是享受美好生活的权利。我想通过一些选择的叠加给予他们更好的生活体验，弥补他们本应拥有，但却失去的权利，对应选择的幻觉这一主题。 

——

In reality, the living space and health conditions of many urban bottom families are very simple, and the narrow space is very crowded. In our daily life, there are many families whose life is just on the line of food and clothing. However, life is equal. Everyone should have the right to choose and enjoy a better life. I want to give them a better life experience through the superposition of some choices, and make up for the lost rights they should have, corresponding to the illusion of choice.
